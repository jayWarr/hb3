Welcome

hummingBird is the poets'  workbench


With hummingBird you can manage your poems:


    • organising,
    • referencing,
    • tagging,
    • selecting,
    • filtering
    • sorting,
    • logging your
      • readings,
      • entries to competitions,
      • submissions to magazines
      • published work.


Important hummingBird is free to use. 


If you find it useful and productive we would welcome a donation to our supported charity

 Medicins Sans Fromtieirs
