  1. How to

use URLs

URLs are used throughout the application to:


    • ensure a direct reference to information,
    • maintain currency
    • obviate the need for entering information (and getting it wrong)
    • for compactness.


Important URLs are best created or changed by copying and pasting the links. This will minimise the likelihood of mistakes which would render the link useless.


The actual clickable URLs (conventionally in blue and underlined) are displayed above their data boxes which are used for editing. URLs should always be entered in full.


Important strict security processes ensure that any attempt to enter bogus information or hacking attempts will be thwarted. All attempts at the latter are automatically logged and reported and will result in the user being  banned from the site and their data deleted.
